Resume Tips

Here are some tips to make a good resume:

  1. Highlight your relevant experience.
    • Include job titles, responsibilities, and achievements.
  2. Use clear and concise language.
    • Avoid jargon and unnecessary details.
  3. Customize your resume for each job application.
    • Focus on the skills and experience that are most relevant to the job.
